University Degrees Dataset - 1,400 Unique Degrees Collection

Comprehensive dataset of 1,400 unique university degrees scraped from LinkedIn, covering renowned institutions across USA, UK, Europe, India, South Korea, Japan, Australia, and Singapore.
November 1, 2024 · 2 min

Overview#

Collected and curated a comprehensive dataset of university degrees by scraping graduation posts from LinkedIn across global institutions.

Dataset Specifications#

Scale#

  • Total Degrees: 1,400 unique degree images
  • Source: LinkedIn graduation posts
  • Geographic Coverage: 8 countries/regions

Geographic Coverage#

  1. USA

    • All renowned Ivy League colleges
    • Top-tier universities
    • Business schools

  2. UK

    • Oxford, Cambridge
    • Russell Group universities
    • Prestigious institutions

  3. Europe

    • Top European universities
    • Leading research institutions

  4. India

    • IITs, IIMs
    • Top national universities
    • Premier institutes

  5. South Korea

    • SKY universities
    • Top research institutions

  6. Japan

    • Tokyo University and peers
    • Leading technical institutes

  7. Australia

    • Group of Eight universities
    • Top research universities

  8. Singapore

    • NUS, NTU
    • Leading Asian institutions

Data Collection Methodology#

Source#

  • LinkedIn Graduation Posts: Students sharing degree images
  • Organic Content: Real, verified degree images
  • Global Reach: Comprehensive international coverage

Process#

  1. LinkedIn Scraping: Using the LinkedIn scraper tool
  2. Query Strategy: Graduation-related keywords
  3. Image Extraction: Downloaded degree certificate images
  4. Deduplication: Ensured unique degrees only
  5. Quality Control: Validated degree authenticity

Dataset Applications#

  • Academic Research: University ranking analysis
  • Educational Trends: Degree program popularity
  • Verification Systems: Automated degree validation
  • Market Research: Educational institution analysis
  • Machine Learning: Training data for document verification
  • Diploma Verification: Fraud detection systems

Value Proposition#

  • Comprehensive: Covers world’s top universities
  • Verified: Real, authentic degree images
  • Diverse: Multiple countries and institutions
  • Unique: No duplicate degrees
  • Large Scale: 1,400+ samples for robust analysis
