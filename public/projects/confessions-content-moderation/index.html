20K+ Confessions Cleaning for Content Moderation

First task at ByteCitadel - cleaned and labeled 20K+ text confessions for content moderation dataset for dip: life beyond colleges app.
March 10, 2024 · 1 min

Overview#

Completed the foundational data cleaning and labeling task for the dip: life beyond colleges social media platform’s content moderation system.

Dataset Details#

  • Volume: 20,000+ confession entries
  • Source: User-submitted confessions
  • Purpose: Content moderation training dataset

Process#

  1. Text Cleaning: Removed special characters, standardized formatting
  2. Data Validation: Ensured data quality and consistency
  3. Labeling: Categorized content for moderation training
  4. Quality Assurance: Validated labeled data for accuracy

Outcome#

Created a clean, labeled dataset ready for training content moderation models, forming the foundation for automated content filtering on the dip social media platform.
